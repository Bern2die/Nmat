Parasympathetic Nervous System

21st July 2020 at 9:41am

The main role of the parasympathetic nervous system is to conserve energy.

It is associated with resting and sleeping states, and acts to reduce heart rate and constrict the bronchi. The parasympathetic nervous system is also responsible for managing digestion by increasing peristalsis and exocrine secretions.

Acetylcholine is the neurotransmitter responsible for parasympathetic responses in the body.
