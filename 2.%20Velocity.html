1.3 Displacement and Velocity

Velocity (v) is a vector. Its magnitude is measured as the rate of change of displacement in a given unit of time, and its SI units are meters per second.

The direction of the velocity vector is necessarily the same as the direction of the displacement vector.

Speed (ν) is the rate of actual distance traveled in a given unit of time.
